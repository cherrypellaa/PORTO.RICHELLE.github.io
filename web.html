Food Blog Website

Richelle Marvela, March 3, 2024




Description

Menu 1: Home
The home page of the food blog features a slideshow showcasing several culinary recommendations in Jakarta. Below the slideshow, there is information about me as the website creator and blog author, along with a preview article on one of Jakarta's cuisines. Each preview article is accompanied by a "Continue Reading" button for visitors to navigate to the detailed explanation page. Additionally, there are three boxes displaying previews of recipes, each with an image of the dish and a "Read More" button to continue reading on another page. Finally, at the bottom, there is a "Contact Us" section containing a map location and a form for visitors to subscribe.




Menu 2: About
The About page provides an explanation about the author, including contact information such as social media handles. Additionally, it includes the "Contact Us" section in the footer.




Menu 3: Recipe
The Recipe menu is divided into four submenus:
1. Indonesian Food
2. Asian Food
3. Western Food
4. Desserts
Each category displays several food menu options along with their corresponding recipes. At the bottom, there is a footer containing the "Contact Us" section.




Menu 4: Jakarta Food
Similarly, the Jakarta Food menu is divided into four submenus:
1. Main Course
2. Desserts
3. Snacks
4. Beverages
Each submenu showcases images of various restaurant choices in Jakarta, categorized by food type, along with detailed explanations about the food offered. The footer includes the "Contact Us" section.




Menu 5: Contact
This page serves as a contact hub, mirroring the information available in the footer section of the website.
